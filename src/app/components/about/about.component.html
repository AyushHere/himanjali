About Me

I’m a guide, space-holder, and intuitive facilitator of healing. Rooted in the wisdom of Ayurveda and Integral Chakra Psychology, I support growth-oriented individuals in reconnecting with their inner truth. As a health coach, I help people identify and release limiting belief patterns, and cultivate healthier, more empowering belief systems. My approach is grounded in radical self-acceptance. When people feel emotionally, mentally, or energetically stuck, I gently guide them to uncover the deeper roots beneath the surface. Whether it’s a blocked pattern, unprocessed emotion, or a disconnection from intuition, we work together to create space for clarity, alignment, and empowered forward movement
